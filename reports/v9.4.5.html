Summary

This release ships a single PM Operations change: entity list filters (Properties, Units, Leases, Owners, and related lists) move to the new list-engine's searchable single-select and multi-select filter inputs, and dependent dropdown scoping between related filters is restored. The change is UI-visible — users filtering these lists will see search boxes inside filter dropdowns and correctly scoped dependent options — but the underlying ticket is a technical follow-up (PMO-2270) to an earlier list-engine capability (PMO-2254).
