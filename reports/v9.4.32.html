Release v9.4.32 — 2026-07-20

View original Slack post · 1784551723.390329

Summary

This release ships 17 changes across PM Operations (voice-transcript reliability, EN↔ES message translation, four Maintenance Dashboard additions, calls-list filters, and drawer/list UX alignment), Tenant Experience & Payments (an onboarding-skip fix that routes eligible residents to the insurance step), Workflows (Command Center chat page context), Leads & Leasing (full lease actions in the Unit drawer, an Extend Portal Access recommendation, and a new applicant payment step), and Banking (three internal Treasury daily-sweep hardening changes). Six items are genuinely customer-visible but not yet covered by an existing Help Center article — most notably the expanding Maintenance Dashboard and the new applicant payment step.

Internal safety check: the daily sweep, return, and watchdog jobs now share a resolver that looks up the platform's Treasury financial account and aborts with a Slack alert if it can't find exactly one match, preventing sweeps from running against an ambiguous account. ⚫ INTERNAL — internal safeguard for a backend job; no customer-facing surface. — no related video
