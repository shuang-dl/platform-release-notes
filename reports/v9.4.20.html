Release v9.4.20 — 2026-07-13

View original Slack post · 1783947176.916589

Summary

Release v9.4.20 ships a maintenance-dashboard insights foundation and property-scoped voice-call permissions in PM Operations; a new Payment Methods settings tab, a Tenants tab in property/unit/lease detail views, insurance reminder and cleanup UI, and lease-list polish in Tenant Experience & Payments; a wave of lease Collection/Lease-Status filters and columns plus the first pieces of the applicant Sign + Pay wizard and a late-fees default in Leads & Leasing; and a daily Banking Slack alert for treasury accounts missing a payment method.
