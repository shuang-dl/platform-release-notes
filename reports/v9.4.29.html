Release v9.4.29 — 2026-07-16

View original Slack post · 1784205423.342109

Summary

This release ships 5 changes across PM Operations (per-tenant renewal recommendations with risk flags), Tenant Experience & Payments (an insurance onboarding step and a new Collection Trend dashboard widget), Leads & Leasing (a recommended-action column on the leases list and embedded HelloSign signing in the applicant portal), and a standalone Tenant Portal move-out access card for staff. Most items are customer-visible and land in existing Help Center collections, with the embedded signing flow standing out as a genuinely new applicant-facing surface with no dedicated article yet.

PMO-2538 Per-tenant renewal recommendations with risk flags Now evaluates every active tenant on a lease and returns an overall renewal recommendation with tenant-level reasons and risk flags. The Renewal Agent now picks a preferred tenant per lease (prioritizing phone number on file and rent-paying status), generates an individual recommendation for every active tenant plus one overall summary, and avoids penalizing non-paying co-tenants (e.g. couples where only one pays) in the score.
