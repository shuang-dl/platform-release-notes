Release v9.4.22 — 2026-07-14

View original Slack post · 1784032326.545049

Summary

This release ships 18 changes across PM Operations (new renewal dashboard insights, task Created-by column, and a task-drawer Communications tab), Tenant Experience & Payments (insurance-dialog snoozing, a new Payment Collection widget, and an overhauled tenants list), Workflows (a generic agent SMS tool and Deposits global search), and Leads & Leasing (Prospect Overview Pipeline/Action-needed/Showings cards and several Unit Drawer polish fixes). Five items are internal-only engineering/design cleanups with no customer-visible surface, and one item (the revamped tenants list) has no clearly matching Help Center article today.
