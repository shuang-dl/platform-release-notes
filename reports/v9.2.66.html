Source: Slack post in #platform-release-notes · posted 2026-05-27 09:14:41 EDT · Bot DevOpsNotifications

Summary

Release v9.2.66 ships 10 changes across PM Operations, Accounting, Onboarding & Imports, Tenant Experience & Payments, Leads & Leasing, and Banking. Customer-facing work is mostly an extension of items already in flight: PMO-988 builds on last week's expense-file owner auto-detection (PMO-605, v9.2.55) by adding a multi-select dropdown, a "set as default" option, and a paperclip-icon refresh on the Owner Portal files page; the Additional Payments screen — broken since launch — now correctly shows alternative payment methods (TEN-397); and a second adoption nudge for Income Verification lands on the Rental Applications screen via a "What's New" dialog. ACT-598 polishes visual inconsistencies in the login/signup fields, including a placeholder that was covering the password value. Everything else is internal: a Renewals Agent API endpoint, a small GSF V2 redirect fix, two Bugsnag noise-reduction tasks, an env-detection refinement, and a Slack alert routing fix.
