ACT-367 Get Started V2 wizard: narrow Zustand subscription to stop re-render storms Improved state selection in the Get Started flow to stabilize predicate data and reduce unnecessary re-renders. In the new Get Started V2 wizard, the usePredicateState hook was subscribing to the entire store — meaning every keystroke into a unit's rent field re-rendered the StepsPanel, Bottombar, progress calculator, and auto-advance logic. The fix replaces the broad subscription with a shallow selector over only the fields the predicates actually read. A user with 50+ units now sees dramatically fewer renders per keystroke. No UX change — same wizard, same behavior, just smoother on larger imports. INTERNAL PERF FIX — no Help Center change
