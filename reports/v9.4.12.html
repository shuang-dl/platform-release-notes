Summary

This release ships one fix in Leads & Leasing: draft leases no longer auto-activate unless the resident has a stored, chargeable payment method on file. This closes a gap where leases could go active without a way to actually collect rent, and it directly affects existing Help Center articles about draft-to-active lease activation and the corresponding training videos on lease creation and the tenant/applicant portal.
