Summary

This release ships 3 changes across Workflows (Tenant Payments now searchable via Global Search with a deep link to the Charges & Payments report), Leads & Leasing (automatic daily reminder emails to re-engage applicants whose income verification has stalled or failed), and Banking (a feature-flag grandfathering change that keeps existing Stripe Treasury accounts on the embedded onboarding/management flow after the flags are turned off for new accounts). Two of the three items likely need Help Center updates; the banking change is an internal flag-rollout with no new user-facing workflow.

Leads & Leasing 1 item

Linear Feature Original Notes (Slack) What it does Affected articles Affected videos
L2L-731 Automatic reminders for stalled income verification Added automatic daily reminder emails to re-engage applicants whose income verification has stalled or failed. A new daily scheduled job automatically emails applicants when their income verification (IV) fails outright or gets stuck mid-flow with no progress for a day, prompting them to resume. Reminders stop once the applicant becomes ineligible or recovers, and the job is idempotent and throttled so applicants aren't double-emailed — reducing the number of stalled applications PMs have to chase manually. Banking 1 item

Linear Feature Original Notes (Slack) What it does Affected articles Affected videos
BAN-1334 Grandfathered Stripe Treasury accounts keep the embedded flow Now supports grandfathered Stripe Treasury accounts in the embedded onboarding and management flow when feature flags are turned off. DoorLoop is turning off the banking-embedded-onboarding-ui and banking-create-stripe-account-with-treasury feature flags so brand-new merchant accounts stop getting the embedded/Treasury flow. An OR-clause grandfathers any merchant account that already has a Stripe Treasury capability, so accounts already on the embedded onboarding/management dialog keep working exactly as before — no visible change for existing users, and no new UI for anyone. INTERNAL — no Help Center change: this is a feature-flag rollback/kill-switch that preserves existing behavior for already-onboarded accounts and doesn't add a new customer-facing capability for new accounts to learn about. Closest existing coverage if reviewed: DoorLoop Wallet: Recipient Banking Setup (Early Access embedded banking article) — no update needed for this ticket specifically. — no related video
