Summary

This release ships 4 changes, all in Tenant Experience & Payments. The two customer-visible items are a first-time card payment discount experiment in the tenant portal (A/B test for eligible one-time payers) and the ability for tenants to mark notifications as read with instant badge count updates. The other two items are internal: an invitation analytics tracking fix and a models migration for shared library infrastructure.

TEN-522 Invitation pageview tracking moved server-side Now tracks invitation pageviews server-side for more reliable login and invite funnel analytics. Moves the invitation_pageview PostHog event from the browser (lossy on mobile/iOS) to the server, so it fires reliably for 100% of tenants who open their invite link — including via SMS and WebView. Fixes an analytics undercount where ~half of invited tenants had no pageview recorded. No change to what tenants see or experience. INTERNAL — analytics fix only; no user-facing behavior changes. No Help Center update needed. — no related video
TEN-243
NFRScalability		 Models migration — task, rental application, eSignature, policy, budget Moved task, rental application, e-signature, policy, and budget models to the shared library for broader reuse. Migrates MongoDB model definitions for tasks, rental applications, eSignature requests/templates, policies, and budgets from the legacy apps/server location to the shared @doorloop/mongodb-models library. This is an architectural refactor to support the decoupled tenant-portal-server. No user-facing change. INTERNAL — infrastructure refactor (NFR/Scalability); no Help Center change needed. — no related video
TEN-490 Mark tenant portal notifications as read Added the ability to mark tenant portal notifications as read, updating unread counts and app badges instantly. Adds a PATCH /notifications/:id/mark-as-read endpoint to the tenant portal server. When a tenant reads a notification, it is marked as read, the unread count is recounted, and a silent push notification updates the app badge instantly. This powers the notification badge staying accurate in the tenant portal mobile app. MATCHED
