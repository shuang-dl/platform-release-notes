Summary

This release ships 4 changes across Onboarding & Imports (1) and Tenant Experience & Payments (3). The tenant-facing highlight is a bug fix restoring accurate stat cards on the Rent Payments Overview dashboard — outstanding balance, collection rate, overdue %, and average days values are now correct. The remaining items are backend improvements: a self-healing Salesforce merchant-account sync, a performance boost for tenant requests with file attachments, and internal analytics tracking for lease e-signature completions.
