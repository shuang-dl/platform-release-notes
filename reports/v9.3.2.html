Summary

This release ships 4 changes across Onboarding & Imports (1) and Tenant Experience & Payments (3). The tenant-facing highlight is a bug fix restoring accurate stat cards on the Rent Payments Overview dashboard — outstanding balance, collection rate, overdue %, and average days values are now correct. The remaining items are backend improvements: a self-healing Salesforce merchant-account sync, a performance boost for tenant requests with file attachments, and internal analytics tracking for lease e-signature completions.

ACT-883 Self-healing merchant account Salesforce sync Now self-heals merchant account syncs when a deleted Salesforce record would otherwise block updates. The SYNC_MERCHANT_ACCOUNT job was failing with Salesforce's INVALID_CROSS_REFERENCE_KEY error when a related record (e.g. a deleted parent Account or stale lookup field) on the merchant account payload no longer existed. The fix detects the dangling reference and self-heals before retrying the update. Only 2 occurrences in 30 days — low volume, no customer-visible impact. ⚫ INTERNAL
Backend Salesforce sync fix — no customer-facing change; no Help Center update needed.		 — no related video
