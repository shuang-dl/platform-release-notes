Summary

This release ships 2 changes: Tenant Experience & Payments gets a tenant-portal contact fix that reflects the company's DoorLoop number for SMS and calls instead of a stale company phone number (PMO-2540), and Leads & Leasing gets a new "Next actions" column on the draft-leases list letting PMs send for e-signature, remind, send a pay link, or activate a lease directly from the list, with built-in 24-hour resend cooldowns (L2L-1573, flag-gated behind flamingo-flow-pm). Both features have existing Help Center coverage to update; no net-new articles are needed for this release.

PMO-2540 Tenant portal now shows your real DoorLoop number Added tenant portal contact settings to show the company's DoorLoop number for SMS and calls when available. When a company has an active DoorLoop number, the tenant portal's Contact tab, dialog, and sidebar now reflect that number as the SMS contact (and, if the voice flag is on, the call number too) instead of falling back to the generic company phone. Each slot still honors its own show/hide toggle; if a PM turns a slot off, it stays hidden. This only applies to tenant logins — owner portal has a separate, sibling change (PMO-2541). MATCHED

L2L-1573 Drive draft leases forward from the list, one click at a time Added a draft leases Next actions column with one-click send, reminder, and activate options plus 24-hour resend cooldowns. The draft-leases list gets a new "Next actions" column that derives the right next step from the lease's status: Send for e-signature, Send pay link, Send reminder, or Activate lease — no more digging into each draft individually. Resending is capped by a 24-hour cooldown, with the last-sent time shown so PMs know when they can nudge a tenant again. Currently gated behind the flamingo-flow-pm flag. MATCHED
