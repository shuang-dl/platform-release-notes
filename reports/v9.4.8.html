Release v9.4.8 — 2026-07-07

View original Slack post · 1783428830.515579

Summary

This release ships 10 changes across PM Operations (per-view preset columns, a combined range filter for currency/percentage custom fields), Tenant Experience & Payments (a new list-engine Leases list, faster tenant-portal login, demo-portal insurance, and a payment-hold fix), and Leads & Leasing (list-engine v2 migrations for embedded Rental Applications and Signature Requests tabs, plus a new draft-lease drawer). One Accounting-labeled item (L2L-1213) is dev-only feature-flag tooling with no customer-facing surface — and appears to be mis-filed under Accounting (see note below).

Mis-cite flag: L2L-1213 ("Added dev-only browser extension overrides for backend feature flags…") is listed under the Accounting header in the Slack post, but its Linear ticket prefix (L2L) and team (Lead To Lease) have nothing to do with Accounting. The content itself — a Chrome-extension-driven backend feature-flag override for local/dev environments — is internal developer tooling regardless of which team header it sits under. Classified as INTERNAL below.

TEN-934 Fix: over-limit tenant payments now held for review instead of blocked Now supports over-limit tenant portal card and eCheck payments when payment review is enabled, so they can proceed to review. Bug fix — with the Payment Hold & Risk Review flag on, a tenant payment that exceeds merchant limits was being rejected outright instead of entering the intended "pending review" hold state. Now it correctly proceeds to a held/pending state and surfaces as a Payment-await incident in DoorWatch, matching the feature's original design. no Help Center change — restores already-documented intended behavior of the Payment Hold & Risk Review flow; no new customer-facing behavior to document — no related video
