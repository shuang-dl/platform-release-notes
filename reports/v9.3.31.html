Release v9.3.31 — 2026-06-30

View original Slack post · 1782823565.845759

Summary

This release ships 23 tracked changes across seven areas: PM Operations leads with a broad rollout of the shared list engine to Vendors, Notes, and Inspections lists plus new Task/Recurring Task drawer overview cards and SMS escalation follow-through; Accounting fixes a stuck reconciliation dialog and adds bill deep-links from search; Tenant Experience & Payments restores dependent filter chaining and lays the foundation for a unified tenant Settings page; Workflows and Leads & Leasing add showings-calendar filters, popups, and a new-showing CTA, plus Bills in global search; and Banking improves micro-deposit verification messaging and merchant-account status visibility. A handful of items (Salesforce RevOps alerting, Get Started V2 monitoring, application-fee analytics instrumentation, and a banking debug endpoint) are internal-only and have no customer-facing Help Center or training impact.
