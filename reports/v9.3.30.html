Release v9.3.30 — 2026-06-29

View original Slack post · 1782739948.183869

Summary

This release ships 19 changes across PM Operations (Owners list redesign, Tasks/Recurring Tasks date-range filters), Accounting (activation-day credit + optional net-due auto-charge at lease signing), Tenant Experience & Payments (Western Union cash payment slips in the tenant portal, Pay-or-Quit email campaigns, a unified tenant invitation email, and Pending Review held payments), Workflows (renewal-trigger publishing, a search-performance fix, and showings on the calendar), Leads & Leasing (a rental-application link label fix, a new Unit drawer Communication tab, and MITS syndication flag scaffolding), and Banking (Stripe failure alerting, a payout-account reminder email, a low-volume alert, and an internal delete action), plus two backend performance improvements under Other.
