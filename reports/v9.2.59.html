Summary

Release v9.2.59 ships 3 changes — two are improvements to the Voice module in the Communications Center (still behind the Voice beta flag): smarter recipient experience when a caller drops mid-screening, and AI-generated suggested-action chips that appear under each call summary so a single tap creates a contact, sends a rental application, or files a task. The third item is a backend reliability fix to the SSO login redirect that eliminates a class of 500 errors. No general-availability features in this release that need Help Center coverage today; the Voice module additions are candidates for a refreshed Voice / AI-summary article once GA.
