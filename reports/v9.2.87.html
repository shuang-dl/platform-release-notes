Summary

This release ships 8 changes across PM Operations (missed-call tracking bugfix, AI SMS signature endpoint), Tenant Experience & Payments (3 internal infrastructure items), Workflows (1 internal infrastructure item), and Leads & Leasing (1 showing UI fix plus 1 no-link UI enhancement). The two most notable customer-facing changes are the fix ensuring missed inbound calls are now reliably tracked in voice reporting, and the AI auto-responder SMS signature being moved from server-side stamping to agent-controlled — meaning AI reply text in Communications Center will look cleaner.

PMO-1556 AI auto-responder SMS signature is now agent-controlled Added an agent SMS signature endpoint and stopped auto-responder texts from adding a server-side AI assistant stamp. Adds a GET /api/communications/agent-sms-signature endpoint that returns the SMS signature the AI agent appends to its messages.
