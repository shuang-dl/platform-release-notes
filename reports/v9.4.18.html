Summary

This is a small, two-feature release. In Tenant Experience & Payments, TEN-988 ships the final approved UI for the tenant-portal insurance-required dialog (desktop & mobile) — a visual/copy refinement on top of the wiring TEN-943 shipped in v9.4.15. In Leads & Leasing, L2L-1881 adds a 3-tab quick-view drawer (Overview, Communication, Reports) for rental applications, letting PMs review an application without leaving the list view.

L2L-1881 Rental application quick-view drawer Added a quick-view drawer for rental applications with overview, communication, and reports tabs. Adds a 3-tab entity drawer (Overview, Communication, Reports) to the v2 rental-applications list. Clicking the quick-view icon opens the drawer over the list — status, action-needed prompts, prospect/assignee cards, and related applications on the Overview tab; prospect-scoped messages on Communication; embedded screening report on Reports. Row-click still navigates to the full application page; the list stays visible behind the drawer. MATCHED
