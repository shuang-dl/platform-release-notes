Summary

This release ships a single change to PM Operations: a pre-send cost summary for Announcements (PMO-206) that shows credits used, remaining balance, and top-up charges before a message goes out. It directly extends the existing Announcements credit/pricing documentation and the "Sending Out Announcements" training video, both of which should be updated to reflect the new pre-send confirmation screen.
