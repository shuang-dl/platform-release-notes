Release v9.4.2 — 2026-07-05

View original Slack post · 1783254759.537649

Summary

This release ships 30 changes across PM Operations (quick-view drawers for vendors/inspections/properties, single-conversation view, friendlier failed-message reasons, automatic credit top-up), Tenant Experience & Payments (a new Tenants list, portal Password and Language & Regional settings tabs, auto-decline of stale held payments, late-fee accuracy fixes, and a demo tenant portal), Accounting (merchant account onboarding fixes and a lease deposits report fix), Onboarding & Imports (a guided trial balance import wizard), Workflows (a richer agent step-builder and permission-aware task search), Leads & Leasing (Action Needed card redesign, lease renewal activation fix, and clearer showing-date labels), and Banking (transfer and treasury-sync reliability work).
