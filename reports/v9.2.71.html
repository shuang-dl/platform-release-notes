Release v9.2.71 — 2026-06-01

View original Slack post · 1780322785.868159 · posted by DevOpsNotifications

Summary

Release v9.2.71 ships 26 changes across seven product areas, with the heaviest investment in PM Operations voice features (9 items) and a multi-line rental-application fee overhaul in Leads & Leasing. Customer-visible highlights include MMS support and unknown-contact auto-assignment in the Communications Center, emergency-contact email on the tenant overview, and a Get Started fix that stops migrated leases from showing phantom security-deposit balances. Most of the voice routing, holding-fee, and banking-notification work is still behind feature flags (beta), and roughly a third of the release is internal NFR/infrastructure hardening with no Help Center impact.
