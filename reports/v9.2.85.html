Release v9.2.85 — 2026-06-08

View original Slack post · 1780925598.938169

Summary

This release ships 10 changes across PM Operations (1), Accounting (1), Onboarding & Imports (3), Tenant Experience & Payments (1), Workflows (2), Leads & Leasing (1), and Other (1). The two headline customer-facing items are offline-first inspection editing on mobile (PMO-1570, behind a feature flag) and saved views + CSV/Excel export for property/unit lists in Accounting. Workflows gains a configurable outstanding-balance threshold for Late Balance Notice triggers (WOR-1090, also flag-gated). The remaining items are internal reliability, monitoring, and infrastructure work.
