Release v9.2.63 — 2026-05-26

Source: Slack post in #platform-release-notes · posted 2026-05-26 10:49 EDT · Bot DevOpsNotifications

Summary

Release v9.2.63 ships 14 changes across PM Operations, Onboarding & Imports, Tenant Experience & Payments, and Leads & Leasing. The headline customer-impact item is Showings is now restricted to Pro and Premium plans — Starter customers will no longer see the showings nav, screens, prospect cards, or comms-center action (L2L-1141). Other customer-facing work: the AI Communications Center auto-responder ships a real settings UI with a first-time dialog and AI-add-on paywall (PMO-1010); the AI tenants resolution feature gets re-platformed as a new Concierge Agent card under Settings → AI Agents (PMO-602); income verification gets a new adoption popup at the end of the unit-listing wizard (L2L-890) and a friendlier translation pass; the rent-payments overview now carries a dismissible "share feedback" banner (TEN-368); and a couple of bug-fix polish items hit work-order email status labels (PMO-998) and property-import owner matching (ACT-412). The remaining 7 items are internal refactors, server-side APIs, schema validation, telemetry, and tracking work — no Help Center impact.

⚠️ High-impact item: L2L-1141 gates the entire Showings feature behind Pro/Premium. Every published Showings article needs a tier-gate notice. See the Leads & Leasing section below.
